Press Kit

Official assets for editorial use. Please don’t alter colors or proportions — the gold crown must remain gold.

Brand usage

  • Use “CheqMate” with a capital C and M.
  • Do not recolor the crown — keep it gold.
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or add effects to the mark/wordmark.
  • Maintain clear space equal to the height of the crown around the logo.
  • Minimum digital size: 24 px height for the mark, 20 px cap height for the wordmark.
  • Backgrounds: use solid dark or light; avoid busy imagery behind the logo.

Downloads

  • Mark — 1024×1024 (PNG, transparent)
  • Wordmark — 1024×512 (PNG, transparent)
  • Mark — 1024×1024 (PNG, opaque)
  • Wordmark — 1024×512 (PNG, opaque)
  • Open Graph image (PNG)
