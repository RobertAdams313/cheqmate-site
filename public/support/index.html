FAQ

Does CheqMate connect to my bank?
No. CheqMate is designed for privacy. You can manually track bills and paychecks without linking accounts.

Which iOS versions are supported?
iOS 18.6 and later. Apple Watch and widgets are supported.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email support@cheqmate.app with steps and screenshots if possible.
