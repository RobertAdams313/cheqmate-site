FAQ

Which iOS versions are supported?

iOS 26 and later. Apple Watch and widgets included.

Does CheqMate connect to my bank?

No. CheqMate is privacy-first. You can plan and track without linking accounts.

How do I join the beta?

Tap Test on TestFlight and follow the prompt.

How do I capture screenshots with the device bezel?
  1. Simulator ▸ Window ▸ Show Device Bezels
  2. File ▸ New Screenshot
  3. PNG saves with bezel — drop into /public/assets/screenshots/
