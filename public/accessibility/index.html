Accessibility Statement

CheqMate is committed to inclusive design. Our iOS app supports Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, Reduce Motion, high contrast, and other system accessibility settings. We follow Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines and WCAG 2.1 AA where practical for a mobile budgeting app.

Known Areas We Monitor

  • Color contrast of text and key UI elements
  • Dynamic Type scaling and readable layouts
  • VoiceOver labels and traits for interactive controls
  • Focus order and hit-target sizes
  • Motion sensitivity and reduced animation paths

Feedback

If you encounter a barrier, please email support@cheqmate.app with details (device, iOS version, steps, screenshots). We aim to respond within 7 business days.
