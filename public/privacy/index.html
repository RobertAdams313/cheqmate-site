Privacy Policy

No tracking. No data sales. Your finances stay private.

CheqMate does not collect personal data. We don’t maintain servers that store your financial information. Your data stays on your devices and (optionally) in your iCloud.

When you use optional features like backups, exports, or iCloud sync, your data remains under your control.
