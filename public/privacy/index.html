Privacy Policy

Effective: September 24, 2025

Overview

CheqMate is designed to keep your financial data private. We do not collect, track, sell, or share your personal data. App data is stored on your device and, if enabled, synced via iCloud under your Apple ID.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registrations with us
  • No analytics SDKs or ad tracking
  • No server-side storage by CheqMate

On-Device & iCloud

Your budgets, bills, and related information are stored locally using Apple frameworks and may sync through iCloud if you have iCloud enabled on your device. You can disable iCloud at any time in iOS Settings.

Notifications & Widgets

Notifications and widgets are powered by iOS system frameworks. Notification content is generated on-device.

Contact

Questions? Email support@cheqmate.app.

Changes

If we update this policy, we’ll change the date above. Material changes will be highlighted in-app and/or on this page.
